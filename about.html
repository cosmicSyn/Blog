A lil' somethin' bout me!

Why do I write?

I find it therapeutic to write. That is why I say it makes life much more bearable. Plus, it shall also (hopefully) help me become an articulate person I aspire to be.


Here’s a thought on writing I’ll leave you with- Writing is a medium of thinking. Usually, it’ll be thought of as being much more goal-oriented, precise, and controlled than the image- laden stream of consciousness or the intrusive internal monologue which is more experiential and chatter-like. But what if the writing exercise could be done in a state of reverie? Do you think that would be possible?

Why I started this blog?

This blog is just a medium to share. Well, you could also call it a public filtered extension of my diary. I think this quote from ‘How to Take Smart Notes’ by Sonke Ahrens conveys the idea perfectly-


An idea kept private is as good as one you never had. And a fact no one can reproduce is no fact at all. Making something public always means to write it down so it can be read. There is no such thing as a history of unwritten ideas.

What do I write about?

I mostly write around philosophy, books, podcasts, music, and personal experiences and learnings. However, I can’t put any specific labels on this blog. And I know this immediately seen as a red flag by most people; they say- “Pick a niche, attract an audience”. And maybe, someday I’ll find my niche and stick to it. But until then, let this be an open-ended exploration into life.



When did I start this blog?

The first post was published on Notion on 8 April 23 after chronic procrastination lasting months. Post that I’ve been lucky to migrate to this simple, yet adorable website developed by my brother.
